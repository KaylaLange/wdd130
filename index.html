Colorado

Colorado is located in the western United States and is a well known Mountain State. Denver, the capital city of Colorado, is the largest city in Colorado and is known as the "Mile High" city due to its high elevation. Colorado is bordered by Wyoming, Nebraska, Kansas, Oklahoma, New Mexico, and Utah. It boasts beautiful mountain landscapes, forests, high plains, mesas, canyons, plateaus, rivers, and desert lands. Colorado is home to a plethora of wildlife, including elk, moose, bighorn sheep, mountain lions, black bears, among many others.
