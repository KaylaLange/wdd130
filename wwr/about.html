History

In the heart of the Rocky Mountains, amidst snow-capped peaks and rushing rivers, a passion for adventure ignited a dream. In 2015, Kayla, a seasoned outdoorswoman with a deep love for the wild beauty of the region, founded Rocky Mountain Raftin. Inspired by the thrill of whitewater rafting and the desire to share it with others, she set out to create a company that would combine exhilarating river experiences with exceptional safety and a profound respect for the environment.

From humble beginnings with a handful of rafts and a crew of passionate guides, Rocky Mountain Rafting has grown into a respected leader in the local rafting community. Their commitment to providing unforgettable adventures while prioritizing guest safety and environmental responsibility has earned them a loyal following of adventure seekers. Today, they continue to push the boundaries of exploration, crafting new rafting trips that showcase the hidden gems and breathtaking landscapes of the Rockies.
