Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to gain brand awareness, educate the audience on the white water rafting experience, and increase revenue through engaging content that drives the audience to book a trip with us.

Audience

Who are the target customers? The demographics most prominant in white water rafting are primarily males in the age range of 13-44, with an emphasis on ages 13-17. This is a sport that appeals to all levels of income and education, with extra appeal to those in a higher income bracket. Families with children 6 and older are also a demographic to be focused on. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? Our audience expects to find pricing information, the schedule of trips provided, frequently asked questions and safety information, what to expect on the day of their trip and how to prepare, a way to book, and a way to contact the company with additional questions. How will my audience usually access my site? The audience will likely be using a laptop or mobile device to access the website.
